The 17 Sustainable Development Goals

A global framework created by the United Nations to address humanity's greatest challenges by 2030.

No Poverty

Ending poverty in all forms everywhere.

Zero Hunger

Achieving food security and sustainable agriculture.

Good Health & Well-being

Ensuring healthy lives for all.

Quality Education

Providing inclusive and equitable education.

Gender Equality

Achieving equality and empowering all people.

Clean Water

Ensuring access to water and sanitation.

Affordable Clean Energy

Expanding access to sustainable energy.

Decent Work & Economic Growth

Promoting sustainable economic opportunities.

Industry & Innovation

Building resilient infrastructure.

Reduced Inequalities

Creating a more inclusive world.

Sustainable Cities

Creating inclusive communities.

Responsible Consumption

Promoting sustainable lifestyles.

Climate Action

Taking urgent action against climate change.

Life Below Water

Protecting marine ecosystems.

Life on Land

Protecting terrestrial ecosystems.

Peace & Justice

Building peaceful societies.

Partnerships

Strengthening global cooperation.
