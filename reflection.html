Project Reflection

A personal reflection on sustainability, research, and how the IB learner can contribute towards achieving the Sustainable Development Goals.

01

Understanding the SDGs

I began this project by exploring why the United Nations created the Sustainable Development Goals and how global challenges require cooperation between governments, organisations, and individuals.

02

Research & Exploration

Researching climate change, education, equality, and innovation revealed how interconnected the SDGs are. A solution to one issue can influence progress in many others.

03

Applying the IB Perspective

The project connects with the IB philosophy by encouraging inquiry, reflection, communication, and responsible action. The SDGs transform learning from theoretical knowledge into meaningful impact.

04

Skills Developed

Through designing this website, I developed skills in:

• Research and source evaluation
• Digital design
• Communication
• Problem solving
• Global awareness
