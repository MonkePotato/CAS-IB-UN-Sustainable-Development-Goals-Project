From Learning To Impact

How IB students can contribute to achieving the Sustainable Development Goals.

Turning IB Learning Into Sustainable Action

The SDGs provide IB students with a framework to transform knowledge into meaningful action through CAS, academic inquiry, and community engagement.

🌱 Environmental Action

Relevant SDGs:

SDG 6 • Clean Water
SDG 12 • Responsible Consumption
SDG 13 • Climate Action
SDG 15 • Life on Land

  • Conduct a school energy audit and suggest ways to reduce electricity consumption.
  • Create a recycling awareness campaign.
  • Organise a school garden project.
  • Perform biodiversity surveys for ESS or Biology.
  • Reduce personal carbon footprint and track progress.

📚 Education & Equality

Relevant SDGs:

SDG 4 • Quality Education
SDG 5 • Gender Equality
SDG 10 • Reduced Inequalities

  • Tutor younger students in academic subjects.
  • Create educational content about sustainability.
  • Organise workshops on digital literacy.
  • Support inclusive activities for different communities.
  • Create awareness campaigns about equality.

💡 Innovation & Entrepreneurship

Relevant SDGs:

SDG 7 • Clean Energy
SDG 8 • Decent Work
SDG 9 • Innovation

  • Develop a sustainability-focused app or website.
  • Design solutions for reducing waste at school.
  • Research renewable energy solutions.
  • Create a social enterprise proposal.
  • Use technology to spread sustainability awareness.

SDGs Across IB Subjects

🌎 ESS

Investigate climate change, ecosystems, resource management and environmental solutions.

🧬 Biology

Explore biodiversity loss, conservation and human impacts on ecosystems.

📖 TOK

Discuss questions such as:

"Who is responsible for solving global problems?"

📊 Economics

Analyse inequality, sustainable development and economic systems.

🌐 Geography

Study urbanisation, population changes and sustainable cities.

✍ Extended Essay

Investigate a sustainability issue through independent research.

What Can I Do Today?

🌍 Reduce Consumption

Buy fewer unnecessary products, reuse materials, repair items, and avoid single-use plastics.

🚲 Sustainable Transport

Walk, cycle, carpool, or use public transport whenever possible.

🍃 Sustainable Food Choices

Reduce food waste, understand food systems, and support responsible consumption.

🗣 Spread Awareness

Use school platforms, presentations, and social media to educate others.
